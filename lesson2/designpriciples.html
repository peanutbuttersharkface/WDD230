Visual Hierarchy

T-mobile

t-mobile.com



Visual Hierarchy is where you see the most important thing as the largest image or text. T-mobile is advertising their watch so having a large picture of the watch makes sense with the important text following in a smaller size.

White Space and Clean Design

Adobe illustrator

adobeillustrator.com



White space lets the pictures and writen content be more easily seen. I feel like Adobe illustrator has done a great job with that. It makes their site feel clean and easy to navigate.

Proximity

Crumble Cookies

crumblecookies.com



Proximity is a way of showing items are related by how close they are to each other. Crumble does a great job by keeping the cookie title close to the cookie and then having enough space between each cookie that their isn't any confusion which title goes to which cookie.
