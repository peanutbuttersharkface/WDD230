Target Audience Description

The target audience for the chamber of commerce site will be local business owners and employees ages 1-100.


Personas

1.Start up business

Name: John Smith
Occupation: Owner of Artisan Bakery
Demographic: 35 years old, lives in Weiser, Idaho. Married with 4 kids and has a middle income. He was a baker for a large company but has left to be closer to family.

John's story:
John has trained in Europe to become an Artisan baker. He and his family has reciently moved here to be closer to John's parents who are needed more help as they age.

What challenges John:
Having his own bakery has been his dream but John, while a very experienced baker, has no idea how to market a new business.

What John needs:
John needs seminar on how to advertise and the opportunity to network with other local business that can use his business or can promote it.

2. Long time business

Name: Jack Homer
Occupation: owner of local feed and grain store.
Demegraphic: Jack is 56, married and has been working in this business for 36 years.

Jack's story:
Jack has lived here his whole life and inheritied he business from his father. Jack is very knowledgable about his business and other people drive farther to come to his store because of this.

What challenges Jack:
Most of Jack's clients are getting on in years and Jack would like to reach a younger crowd of people. He feels that having a social media presence and a website would be a lot of help. Jack doesn't know much about doing this.

What Jack needs:
Jack needs a semiar on creating and running a website and social media. He could also use suggestions of local people that he could hire to do this as well. He would also be helped with networking and talking to other business owners about how social media and a website could be used to build up his clientel.

Scenarios

1. How to find information about the town and the events that are happening?

2.How to join the chamber, and what membership dues are and what you get with your membership?

3.What businesses are in the area and where are they located?

4. What level of memberships are there and which would be best for my company?

5. How do I contact the Chamber of Commerce if I have questions?
