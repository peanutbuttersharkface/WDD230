About Fruity Blues

Original Store

In 1955 Stan and Sara had a dream to share the best and freshest fruit and vegetables to locals and tourists alike. Stan grew up on a fruit farm in southern California and enjoyed every minute of it. He especially enjoyed having people come to visit the farm and loved how excited they were when they would taste the just picked fresh fruit. When Stan got older, he knew he wanted to bring this experience to more people without leaving the beautiful beach. Stan and Sara opened there first market June 5, 1957, and called it, South Side Market. As the years passed this became a fun family business that everyone that visited Carlsbad made sure they visited the market to try the best fruit California had to offer. In 1990 Stan and Sara decided to start a juice and smoothie stand closer to the beach. They bought some carts and hired a few college students to help them start what was to soon revolutionize their business. They rename their business to Fruity Blues and have never looked back. They no longer have carts on the beach but food trucks that stay close by the beaches in Carlsbad. In 2004 Stan and Sara retired and turned the business over to their children. They remodeled the market and turned it information a smoothie shop to better accommodate the large amount of smoothie orders.
