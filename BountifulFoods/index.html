California Coast

Come get a smoothy and take it to one of the beautiful beaches in Carlsbad. Our shop and each food truck has infromation about the beaches in the area but, here is a run down of a few in Carlsbad.



Tamarack Beach

Also known as Carlsbad State Beach is a great beach for walking and swimming. There are restrooms and lifeguards all summer.



Carlsbad City Beach


If you want to go surfing or watch them, this is the beach for you. Surfing is only May - October so keep that in mind. Lifewuards are on duty during the summer so go ahead and take a dip while you are there.
